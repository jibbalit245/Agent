## Euclidean & Classical Geometry

### References
- **Euclid's Elements** (free online) — https://mathcs.clarku.edu/~djoyce/java/elements/toc.html
  The original, with hyperlinked proofs. Clay Mathematics edition.

- **Coxeter** — Introduction to Geometry
  The modern standard for classical geometry. Projective, affine, inversive, hyperbolic.

- **Berger** — Geometry (2 vols)
  Encyclopedic treatment of classical and modern geometry.

- **MathWorld Geometry** — https://mathworld.wolfram.com/topics/Geometry.html
  Quick lookups: triangle centers, circle theorems, polygon properties, coordinates.

- **Cut the Knot** — https://www.cut-the-knot.org/
  Interactive proofs of classical theorems. Exceptional collection.

- **Clark University Geometry Archive** — https://mathcs.clarku.edu/~djoyce/geometry.html

## Differential Geometry

### Textbooks (Graduate Level)
- **do Carmo** — Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces
  The classic intro to surface theory. Requires only calculus.

- **Lee** — Introduction to Smooth Manifolds (2nd ed)
  Free lecture notes available. The most accessible graduate text.
  Author's page: https://sites.math.washington.edu/~lee/Books/ISM/

- **Lee** — Introduction to Riemannian Manifolds
  Sequel to ISM. Geodesics, curvature, comparison theorems.

- **Spivak** — A Comprehensive Introduction to Differential Geometry (5 vols)
  The complete reference. Volume 1 is most used.

- **Kobayashi & Nomizu** — Foundations of Differential Geometry (2 vols)
  The authoritative advanced reference.

- **Chern, Chen & Lam** — Lectures on Differential Geometry

### Specific Topics
- **Hamilton/Perelman** — Ricci Flow (key papers via arXiv)
- **Milnor** — Morse Theory (gradient flows on manifolds)
- **Guillemin & Pollack** — Differential Topology (transversality, integration on manifolds)

## Algebraic Geometry

### References
- **Hartshorne** — Algebraic Geometry
  The graduate standard. Schemes, cohomology, curves.

- **Vakil** — The Rising Sea (free PDF, updated)
  https://math.stanford.edu/~vakil/216blog/FOAGnov1817public.pdf
  Most pedagogically clear modern algebraic geometry text.

- **Shafarevich** — Basic Algebraic Geometry (2 vols)
  Classical varieties approach.

- **Liu** — Algebraic Geometry and Arithmetic Curves (graduate)

- **Stacks Project** — https://stacks.math.columbia.edu/
  Collaborative algebraic geometry reference. 7000+ pages, fully rigorous.

## Topology with Geometric Flavor

- **Hatcher** — Algebraic Topology (free)
  https://pi.math.cornell.edu/~hatcher/AT/AT.pdf
  Homotopy, homology, cohomology. Standard grad text.

- **Milnor** — Topology from the Differentiable Viewpoint
  Slim, clean, excellent. Brouwer degree, framed cobordism.

- **Thurston** — Three-Dimensional Geometry and Topology
  Geometrization conjecture context. Hyperbolic geometry.

- **Munkres** — Topology (for set-topology foundations)
  https://www.amazon.com/Topology-2nd-James-Munkres/dp/0131816292

## Computational Geometry

### Libraries & Tools
- **CGAL** (Computational Geometry Algorithms Library) — https://www.cgal.org/
  C++ library. Convex hulls, Delaunay triangulation, Voronoi, mesh generation.

- **Shapely** (Python) — https://shapely.readthedocs.io/
  2D geometric objects, set operations.

- **Open3D** (Python/C++) — https://www.open3d.org/
  3D data processing, point clouds, meshes.

- **Geometric Tools** — https://www.geometrictools.com/
  C++ implementations of computational geometry algorithms with documentation.

- **LEDA** — https://www.algorithmic-solutions.com/leda/
  Algorithmic library including geometry.

### Textbooks
- **de Berg et al.** — Computational Geometry: Algorithms and Applications
  https://www.cs.uu.nl/geobook/ (partial free access)
  The standard textbook. Convex hull, line segment intersection, triangulation.

- **O'Rourke** — Computational Geometry in C (free online)
  https://cs.smith.edu/~jorourke/books/compgeom.html

- **Preparata & Shamos** — Computational Geometry: An Introduction

- **Boissonnat & Yvinec** — Algorithmic Geometry

## Discrete & Combinatorial Geometry

- **Matoušek** — Lectures on Discrete Geometry
  Convexity, Radon/Helly theorems, discrepancy, geometric graph theory.

- **Grünbaum** — Convex Polytopes (2nd ed)

- **Ziegler** — Lectures on Polytopes (partial free)
  https://www.math.fu-berlin.de/~ziegler/

- **Pach & Agarwal** — Combinatorial Geometry

## Hyperbolic & Non-Euclidean Geometry

- **Cannon, Floyd, Kenyon, Parry** — Hyperbolic Geometry (free)

- **Ratcliffe** — Foundations of Hyperbolic Manifolds

- **Anderson** — Hyperbolic Geometry (Springer Undergraduate)
