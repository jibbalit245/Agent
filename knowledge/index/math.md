## Encyclopedic References

- **Wolfram MathWorld** — https://mathworld.wolfram.com/
  16,000+ entries with proofs, examples, formulas.
  Subtopics: Algebra, Analysis, Calculus, Combinatorics, Geometry, Number Theory, Topology, etc.

- **NIST DLMF** (Digital Library of Mathematical Functions) — https://dlmf.nist.gov/
  The authoritative reference for special functions (Bessel, Legendre, hypergeometric, etc.)
  Successor to Abramowitz & Stegun. Free, searchable.

- **OEIS** (Online Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences) — https://oeis.org/
  300,000+ sequences with formulas, programs, references.

- **Wikipedia Mathematics Portal** — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:Mathematics
  Good starting point with links to rigorously sourced articles.

- **ProofWiki** — https://proofwiki.org/
  Formal statement-proof format for thousands of theorems.

- **nLab** — https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/HomePage
  Category theory, homotopy theory, type theory. Graduate/research level.

- **Encyclopedia of Mathematics (Springer)** — https://encyclopediaofmath.org/
  Free online mathematical encyclopedia.

- **Project Euclid** — https://projecteuclid.org/

  Includes: Duke Math Journal, Annals of Applied Probability, many more.

- **zbMATH Open** — https://zbmath.org/
  Mathematical reviews database. 4M+ entries.

- **AMS Mathematical Reviews** — https://mathscinet.ams.org/
  Comprehensive (institutional access) but brief reviews are free.

## Graduate Textbooks by Area

### Analysis
- **Rudin** — Principles of Mathematical Analysis (Baby Rudin)
  The definitive real analysis text at undergrad→grad level.
- **Rudin** — Real and Complex Analysis (Big Rudin)
- **Stein & Shakarchi** — Princeton Lectures in Analysis (4 vols) — most accessible grad analysis
- **Folland** — Real Analysis (measure theory, functional analysis)
- **Conway** — Functions of One Complex Variable

### Linear Algebra & Abstract Algebra
- **Axler** — Linear Algebra Done Right (conceptual, no determinants first)
- **Hoffman & Kunze** — Linear Algebra (classic, rigorous)
- **Dummit & Foote** — Abstract Algebra (comprehensive grad reference)
- **Lang** — Algebra (grad standard, advanced)
- **Hungerford** — Algebra

### Topology
- **Munkres** — Topology (standard undergrad-grad topology text)
- **Hatcher** — Algebraic Topology (free PDF)
  https://pi.math.cornell.edu/~hatcher/AT/AT.pdf
- **Bredon** — Topology and Geometry

### Differential Geometry
- **do Carmo** — Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces (classic intro)
- **do Carmo** — Riemannian Geometry
- **Lee** — Introduction to Smooth Manifolds (most accessible grad text)
- **Kobayashi & Nomizu** — Foundations of Differential Geometry (advanced)

### Number Theory
- **Hardy & Wright** — An Introduction to the Theory of Numbers
- **Ireland & Rosen** — A Classical Introduction to Modern Number Theory
- **Neukirch** — Algebraic Number Theory
- **Serre** — A Course in Arithmetic

### Probability & Statistics
- **Billingsley** — Probability and Measure
- **Durrett** — Probability: Theory and Examples (grad, free PDF)
  https://www.math.cornell.edu/~durrett/PTE/PTE5_011119.pdf
- **Williams** — Probability with Martingales
- **Lehmann & Romano** — Testing Statistical Hypotheses

### Combinatorics & Discrete
- **Stanley** — Enumerative Combinatorics (free PDF)
  https://math.mit.edu/~rstan/ec/
- **van Lint & Wilson** — A Course in Combinatorics
- **Lovász** — Combinatorial Problems and Exercises

### Optimization
- **Rockafellar** — Convex Analysis
- **Boyd & Vandenberghe** — Convex Optimization (free)
  https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/
- **Nocedal & Wright** — Numerical Optimization

## Computation & Tools

### Computer Algebra Systems
- **SageMath** (free) — https://www.sagemath.org/
  Python-based. Algebra, number theory, combinatorics, differential geometry.
  Online: https://sagecell.sagemath.org/

- **Mathematica / Wolfram Language** — https://www.wolfram.com/mathematica/
  Industry gold standard. Wolfram Alpha is the free version.

- **Maple** — https://www.maplesoft.com/

- **Maxima** (free) — https://maxima.sourceforge.io/
  Symbolic computation. Lisp-based.

- **GAP** (free, group theory) — https://www.gap-system.org/

- **PARI/GP** (free, number theory) — https://pari.math.u-bordeaux.fr/

### Numerical Libraries
- **NumPy/SciPy** — https://numpy.org/ / https://scipy.org/
- **SymPy** (symbolic) — https://www.sympy.org/
- **MPFR** (arbitrary precision) — https://www.mpfr.org/
