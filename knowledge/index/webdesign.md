## Research & Evidence-Based Design

### UX Research Organizations
- **Nielsen Norman Group** — https://www.nngroup.com/
  200+ research-backed UX articles. Annual conference. Gold standard practitioner research.
  Quantitative benchmarks, eye-tracking studies, usability reports.

- **Baymard Institute** — https://baymard.com/
  45,000+ hours of UX research. E-commerce checkout, forms, search.
  Premium reports but free articles at https://baymard.com/blog

- **Conversion Rate Experts** — https://conversion-rate-experts.com/
  Case studies on A/B testing and CRO.

- **CX Report (Google/Ipsos)** — https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/consumer-insights/consumer-trends/
  Consumer experience quantitative research.

### Academic Research
- **ACM CHI Conference** — https://chi.acm.org/
  Top HCI venue. Papers: https://dl.acm.org/sig/sigchi
- **ACM UIST** — https://uist.acm.org/
  Novel UI technology and interaction techniques
- **Behaviour & Information Technology** (Taylor & Francis)
- **International Journal of Human-Computer Studies** (Elsevier)
- **Interacting with Computers** (Oxford)

## Design Systems & Standards

### Component Libraries & Design Systems
- **Google Material Design 3** — https://m3.material.io/
  Complete design system. Components, tokens, guidelines.

- **Apple Human Interface Guidelines** — https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/
  iOS, macOS, watchOS, visionOS. Interaction principles.

- **Microsoft Fluent Design** — https://fluent2.microsoft.design/
  Windows, Office, cross-platform.

- **Carbon Design System (IBM)** — https://carbondesignsystem.com/
  Enterprise-grade. Open source.

- **Shopify Polaris** — https://polaris.shopify.com/ (e-commerce focused)
- **Atlassian Design System** — https://atlassian.design/
- **Spectrum (Adobe)** — https://spectrum.adobe.com/
- **Base Web (Uber)** — https://baseweb.design/
- **Lightning (Salesforce)** — https://lightningdesignsystem.com/

### Web Standards
- **MDN Web Docs** — https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web
  HTML, CSS, JavaScript — the definitive free reference.

- **W3C Standards** — https://www.w3.org/standards/
  HTML living standard, CSS specs, ARIA, WCAG.

- **WHATWG HTML Living Standard** — https://html.spec.whatwg.org/
  The canonical HTML specification.

- **CSS Specifications** — https://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/specs.en.html
  All CSS modules: Grid, Flexbox, Variables, animations.

## Accessibility (A11y)

- **WCAG 2.2** (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) — https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/
  Legal standard in most jurisdictions. A/AA/AAA levels.

- **WAI-ARIA 1.2** — https://www.w3.org/TR/wai-aria-1.2/
  Roles, states, properties for assistive technology.

- **A11y Project** — https://www.a11yproject.com/
  Practical accessibility checklist and patterns.

- **WebAIM** — https://webaim.org/
  Testing tools, screen reader guides, WAVE browser extension.

- **Inclusive Design Principles** — https://inclusivedesignprinciples.org/

- **Gov.uk Design System** — https://design-system.service.gov.uk/
  Accessibility-first. Extensively tested with real users.
